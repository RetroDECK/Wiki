## Naming of Reiki

**English:** Reiki 

**Japanese Katakana:** レイキ 

**Japanese Kanji:** 霊姫

**Chinese:** 灵姬

### What does the name mean? 

Reiki in Chinese 灵姬 is literally "Clever Maiden".

灵 means Clever or Spiritual.

姬 is Princess, and is commonly used as a fancy way to mention Girls (implied maiden/princess/fair lady).

姬 shares its pronunciation with 机.

机 is literally Machine, and can be used to imply anything technology related.

灵机 means Cleverness and Inspiration.

originally used to describe ingenious improvised playing of music instruments.
