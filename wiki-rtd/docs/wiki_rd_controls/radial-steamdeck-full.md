# RetroDECK - Full Radial Menu System

### Is there a quick way to go back to the top of the radial menu system?

Yes, simply press one of the following buttons on the `HKB` trigger buttons: `L4`, `R4`, or `Select`.

---

## Radial Menus

Below is a breakdown of the **Full Radial Menu System** that you can access on the **Steam Deck**. To use the radial menu:

- **Steam Deck**: Use the `Left Touchpad` to bring up the radial menu. Then, press the touchpad to activate the selected radial button.

### Column Definitions

- **Radial Button**: Describes what action the hotkey performs.
- **Keyboard Command**: Shows what key or combination is sent to the component.
- **System Support**: Lists the systems or components that support the command.
- **Icon**: Displays the corresponding icon for the action.
- **Comment**: Provides any additional information or comments regarding the hotkey.

---
